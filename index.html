Maddie Dollins is one of the smartest people I know. She is inquisitive, kind, thoughtful and deliberate. Sometimes too deliberate. Even though many choices in our lives should be shared, examined and discussed, and we gain a sense of community and mutual understanding through these kinds of excercies in empathetic thinking, questions like "Should I get milk?", "What if they are out of chocolate?", and "Should I throw my milk away?" are probably better left for the indivdual to decide. Thus, for those who always carefully consider every decision, there is the WSMD ("What Should Maddie Do?") generator, availible on all devices + browsers. For more work, check out my github!
